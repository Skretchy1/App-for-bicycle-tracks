Welcome to Kolesar.si!

Welcome to the webiste where you can inform, learn and discover new technologies!

On this website you will find a lot of usefull information about the new and upcoming news about cycling.
You also have many options to choose, from grading and discussing, to talking with friends, and learning new stuff.
Our team will help you to get the experience we want you to have, if there are any difficulties, freely contact us.

Search trails or create your own trail on our website! You can input your start point and your end point and it will give you the fastest route possible. Also you can give us your feedback about the trail and we are going to make our efforts to make it even better, so if you have time leave a comment our two, it helps us alot!
Feel free to ask us anything it comes to your mind our team of experts are going to try and resolve your dilemas. You can also see what other users were asking before you and answers from our workers.
We also offer our own advices on how our site works. There you can find some frequently asked questions too. It should help you if you have some issues with using our trail system. If that doesn't help you contact us directly and we will respond to you as soon as possible.
